Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I track my medication?

To track your medication, go to the "Pills" section of the app, and follow the instructions to add a new medication.

What happens if I miss an appointment?

If you miss an appointment, the app will notify you and suggest rescheduling options based on your calendar.

How do I upload documents?

Documents can be uploaded in the "Documents" section. Click "Upload" and select the file from your device.

What should I do if the app crashes?

Try restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact support.
